Implement your own

Fuzzy is designed to allow easy addition of custom implementations to its building blocks, such as attacks, classifiers, and mutators. Click the relevant banner to learn more about how to do it yourself.

ATTACKS
An attack handler modifies prompts and attempts to jailbreak an LLM.
CLASSIFIERS
Classifiers assess responses from LLMs and determine whether they meet certain criteria.
MUTATORS
Mutators modify a given input with the primary goal of neutralizing adversarial prompts.
